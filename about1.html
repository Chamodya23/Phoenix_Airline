At the time, American, TWA, Frontier, and Western were among the airlines that flew into Phoenix. The Super Constellation, DC-3, and DC-6 were common aircraft. Later, Bonanza Airlines, United, and Delta Airlines started operations after Western Airlines had flown Lockheed Electras into Phoenix.

Who We Are

25 Years of Aviation Excellence - Phoenix Aviation first opened its doors in 1994 and now provides executive charter flights all over the world, international air ambulance service, aircraft management services, and aircraft repair and maintenance. For the past 25 years, we have provided this service, and we are prepared for many more years of greatness.

Mission

Our mission is to support Sri Lanka's Green Vision and sustainability drive in order to create a sustainable environment, a sustainable future, and a carbon-neutral nation.

Vision

Empowering the youth of Sri Lanka towards a green and sustainable future by providing unparalleled services for sustainability.

Philosophy

We believe in creating a more environmentally friendly and long-term future. We are dedicated to empowering our stakeholders and discerning citizens to ensure the planet's long-term sustainability.
